About the Book

16-year-old Max Roberts is an average teen in the Republic. He has friends, he flies and he goes to training school. Until a flight goes wrong and he lands in an enemy country.

Now he tries to fit into this new country, and he starts to ask questions—stuff the government would never tell him. Where do his loyalties lie? Who is this mysterious new group called The Remnant?

Will he find out? Or will a hidden enemy take them all out?
